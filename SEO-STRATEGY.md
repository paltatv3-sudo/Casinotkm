## 🔍 SEO Optimizasyon

### 1. On-Page SEO ✅

**Meta Tags:**
- ✅ Title tags (her dil için optimize)
- ✅ Meta descriptions
- ✅ H1, H2, H3 başlıkları
- ✅ Alt text (görüntüler için)
- ✅ Internal linking

**Schema Markup:**
- ✅ Schema.org JSON-LD
- ✅ Organization schema
- ✅ BreadcrumbList schema
- ✅ Product schema (oyunlar)

**Content:**
- ✅ Keyword research (her ülke/dil)
- ✅ Long-tail keywords
- ✅ Natural keyword placement
- ✅ Content length: 1000+ kelime

### 2. Technical SEO ✅

**Site Structure:**
- ✅ Hiyerarşik URL yapısı
- ✅ Sitemap.xml
- ✅ robots.txt
- ✅ Canonical URLs
- ✅ Mobile responsiveness
- ✅ Page speed optimization
- ✅ SSL/HTTPS

**Indexing:**
- ✅ Google Search Console
- ✅ Bing Webmaster Tools
- ✅ Yandex.Webmaster (Rusya)
- ✅ Baidu Webmaster (China)

## ⚡ Hızlı Ranking İpuçları

1. **Mobile First Indexing**
   - Mobile site speed < 3 sn
   - Touch-friendly buttons
   - Mobile navigation

2. **Core Web Vitals**
   - LCP: < 2.5s
   - FID: < 100ms
   - CLS: < 0.1

3. **Content Quality**
   - Original content
   - E-A-T (Expertise, Authority, Trust)
   - User engagement

4. **Backlinks**
   - High domain authority
   - Relevant anchors
   - Natural link profile

5. **Social Signals**
   - Consistent posting
   - User engagement
   - Viral potential
